What is bioinformatics?

Bioinformatics is something scientists use to analyze the sequence of biologial proteins. It is effective in finding patterns in DNA in order to find out what purpose they serve.

Without bioinformatics, we wouldn't have been able to map the entire human genome!

How do these activities apply to bioinformatics?

In bioinformatics, something that you analyze is the frequency of particular amino acid codons, and this can help when identifying associations between amino acids.

Bioinformatics is also used to identify mutations in the genome.
