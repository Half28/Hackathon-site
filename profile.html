About this site

This site was made for the 2020 NucleoTide Hackathon. Although there were multiple prompts, we saw a niche for education on the topic of bioinformatics. We decided to use Qoom for our website, as it was the easiest method to create a front-end website with. Special thanks to Kiae for promptly answering any questions we might have had!
